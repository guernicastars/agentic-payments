\section{Risks}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Real data thin or unavailable:} synthetic carries the demo; we frame as "validated on 5 ground-truth-labelled policy classes; same pipeline runs on Base."
  \item \textbf{Interpretability probe doesn't converge in time:} the composite-score "explanations" come from feature-attribution rules (top-3 z-scores per flagged wallet), no probe needed for the demo.
  \item \textbf{UMAP separation muddier than expected:} keep the t-SNE fallback ready; tune perplexity; if both fail, switch to PCA + isolation-forest on raw features.
\end{itemize}
